03 // Projects

Signature
Field Work

PROJECT_01
Airborne LiDAR Survey — Sagarnath Forest Area

Helicopter-mounted LiDAR covering ~280 km² at 30–80 pts/m². Produced high-res orthophotos, DSM, DTM, 0.5m/1m contours. Deployed GCPs with DGPS for ±8–12 cm vertical accuracy validation.

Airborne LiDAR DTM/DSM DGPS 280 km²
PROJECT_02
Bagmati Irrigation Project Topographic Mapping

Large-scale 900 km² airborne LiDAR survey for irrigation infrastructure planning. Full pipeline: flight planning, GNSS base station, PPK trajectory, strip adjustment, to final DTM and orthophoto deliverables.

900 km² Airborne LiDAR PPK Irrigation
PROJECT_03
UAV-LiDAR Survey — 50 MW Solar PV Site, Rolpa

DJI Matrice 300 RTK with Penmuse P1 + H600 LiDAR for solar site planning. Generated 3D point clouds, DEM, DSM, and orthomosaic using LiGeoreference, LiDAR360, and DJI Terra.

UAV LiDAR Solar PV DJI M300 RTK Orthomosaic
PROJECT_04
Bathymetric Survey — Phewa & Kulekhani Lakes

RTK-integrated echo sounding for detailed underwater terrain modeling. Fused UAV imagery with bathymetric data for seamless 3D lake terrain. Sedimentation volume analysis for Kulekhani reservoir capacity assessment.

Bathymetry RTK Echo-Sounder Sedimentation 3D Modeling
PROJECT_05
Flood Risk Assessment — Chandrauta Solar Site

Comprehensive hydrological study for proposed solar site. GIS-based peak discharge modeling, catchment analysis, and drainage delineation. Produced flood risk maps to guide site planning and mitigation.

Flood Modeling GIS Hydrology UAV DEM Risk Mapping
PROJECT_06
3D Heritage Documentation — Krishna Mandir (TLS)

High-precision FARO Focus Premium terrestrial laser scanning of Krishna Mandir heritage temple. Multi-position scan registration in FARO Scene with noise filtering and georeferencing for digital heritage conservation.

TLS FARO Scene Heritage BIM Point Cloud
