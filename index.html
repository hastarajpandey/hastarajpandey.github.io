About Me

I'm a Geomatics Engineer and Senior Surveyor based in Kathmandu, Nepal, holding a Diploma in Geomatics Engineering and a Bachelor of Arts in Geography. With over five years in the field, I specialize in turning raw terrain data into precise, decision-ready geospatial deliverables.

"Mapping the unmapped — one point cloud at a time."

My expertise spans the complete geospatial pipeline: from helicopter-mounted LiDAR acquisition over hundreds of square kilometres, to millimetre-precision terrestrial laser scanning of heritage structures, to bathymetric surveys of Nepal's critical reservoirs.

I'm drawn toward integrating machine learning and advanced remote sensing for land surface dynamics, environmental monitoring, and infrastructure planning — with a growing focus on AI-assisted change detection and flood risk modelling.
